Sales Insights Dashboard

Key Insights:

This Power BI dashboard provides insights into sales performance across different regions, product categories, and time periods. The key findings include:

  • Highest Sales Region: North America shows the highest sales growth year over year.
  • Top Product Category: Electronics continues to be the top-selling category, contributing 40% of the total sales.
  • Quarterly Trends: Sales peak in Q4 due to holiday promotions.

The visualizations are designed to give actionable insights for the marketing and sales teams.
